01

Ownership from day one.

You join early enough that your decisions shape the product, not just the backlog. Whole surfaces are yours to own — from the first sketch to what ships and how it’s measured.

How we hire

We hire the way we tell others to.

We run our own process through Spine — behaviour over pedigree, evidence over vibes.

Expect a short, structured process built around the work, not a CV scan. We seal our read before we open Spine’s, look at what you’ve actually shipped and owned, and name the one risk worth probing before we decide. You’ll be judged on a real task, not a vibe in a room.

It’s the same standard we hold our product to — and it cuts both ways. You’ll see exactly what we’re reading and why, and we hold ourselves to the call once you’re in the seat.
