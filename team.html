How we hire

We run Spine on ourselves.

The bar we hold candidates to is the bar the product was built to enforce — so every offer here is sealed, evidenced and reviewed the same way.

01

Behaviour over pedigree.

The CV gets you read, never hired. We weigh what someone has actually shipped, owned and recovered from — not the logos behind it. The person who interviews best is rarely the person who delivers best.

02

Evidence over vibes.

Every call is backed by something we can point to in the work record. No flag without a reason, no offer on gut alone — the same discipline Spine forces on the customers we serve.

03

Ownership, not seat-warming.

Each seat owns an outcome end to end. We hire people who close their own loops and don’t need the gap pointed out twice — autonomy is the whole reason the team stays small.

04

Speed with a record.

We move fast and then check our work. Every prediction we make — about a hire, a feature, a customer — gets held against reality, so the loop that teaches us actually closes.
