FAQ

Pricing, answered.

Is there a free trial?
Better than a trial — the setup is free for good. You can sign up, connect your tools and build your full pipeline view at no cost, with no card and no time limit. You only pay once you want Spine to unlock decision review and pipeline scoring on that pipeline. So you see the whole product stood up before any money changes hands.
What counts toward my hires?
Tiers are sized by the number of hires you make in a year — Entry for 1–3, Growth for up to 10, Scale for 25+, Platform for funds and portfolios. A "hire" is a role you take through to a decision and verify post-offer; building your pipeline, screening and exploring candidates never counts. If your hiring volume changes, you move tiers — you're never charged per seat or per candidate.
Can I cancel any time?
Yes. Spine is month-to-month with no lock-in — cancel any time. Your integrations and pipeline view stay free even if you stop paying; you simply lose decision review and scoring until you reactivate. Annual billing is offered at a discount for teams that want it, but it's never required.
What do I get for free?
Everything up to the decision itself. You get a free account, all the integrations (Greenhouse, Lever, Gmail, Slack) and a live pipeline view built from your connected tools. What sits behind the paywall is the intelligence layer — the gut-vs-Spine read, pipeline scoring, risk flags and post-hire verification at Day 30/60/90/180.
How does annual billing work?
Pay for twelve months up front and you get 20% off the monthly rate on every tier — Entry at £156/mo, Growth at £396/mo, Scale at £1,000/mo, and Platform from £2,360/mo. Toggle "Annual" above to see the equivalent monthly price and the yearly total for each plan. Monthly billing is always available if you'd rather stay flexible.
